About

This was a team project for my Adavnced Programming Techniques class at Princeton.

This Thesis Advisor Search Engine allows students to browse and search through Princeton professors in the ORFE department, see their bio, past papers they advised and their contact infomation. Then the students can add the professors to their preference form and submit the form all from the same website.

On the admin side, you can add, delete and edit professors, view student preferences and get suggested student-professor pairings.
